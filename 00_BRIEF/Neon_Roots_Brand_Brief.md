# PROJECT BRIEF: Neon Roots
**Client:** Neon Roots Urban Farming
**Mission:** Rebrand urban hydroponics as high-tech, cyberpunk lifestyle.

## Core Ask
We need a full brand package for a direct-to-consumer hydroponic basil and microgreen subscription service. 

## Target Audience
- Tech workers in dark apartments.
- Cyberpunk enthusiasts.
- Health-conscious gamers.

## Deliverables
1.  **User Personas:** Who buys UV-blasted basil?
2.  **Brand Aesthetics:** Dark mode, neon greens, industrial fonts.
3.  **Web Concept:** A landing page that looks like a terminal hack.
