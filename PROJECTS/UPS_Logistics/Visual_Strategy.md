## Visual Strategy

**Logo Prompt:** Create a modern, professional logo for a project titled "UPS Global Supply Chain Logistics Dashboard". Focus on minimalism and a vector style with a white background. The logo should incorporate elements representing global logistics, real-time data, and optimization. Use UPS' branded colors: Brown (#351C15) and Gold (#FFB500). The design should convey innovation and efficiency, aligning with the project's goals of enhancing shipment visibility, delay prediction, route optimization, and KPI monitoring.

**Mockup Prompt:** Design a high-fidelity landing page UI mockup for a "UPS Global Supply Chain Logistics Dashboard" with a focus on a "Modern UI, Clean, Dribbble style, Hero Section, Dark Mode". The dashboard is designed to enhance global logistics visibility and management, allowing logistics managers to visualize shipments, predict delays, and optimize routes in real-time. Include the following elements: a collapsible sidebar to maximize map area, an interactive globe-based visualization tool, AI-driven alert notifications, a route optimization feature, and a KPI dashboard. Use UPS' branded colors: Brown (#351C15) and Gold (#FFB500). Aim for a professional, data-dense design with high contrast for clarity. The mockup should reflect brand consistency and align with the advanced, precise nature of the user's tasks.
