# User Personas for DEEP_SPACE

## Persona 1: Captain Olivia Tesla

**Bio:**
Captain Olivia Tesla is a seasoned space logistics coordinator working with the United Earth Trade Federation. She has extensive experience in handling interstellar cargo and managing large transport fleets. Olivia is known for her problem-solving skills and calm demeanor, even during high-stress situations. She holds a degree in Astrophysics and has spent over a decade working in various space stations, coordinating vital supply chains.

**Goals:**
- Monitor cargo shipments in real-time to ensure the efficiency and safety of transport routes.
- Quickly assess and address potential risks like piracy or mechanical failures.
- Maintain open communication with drone pilots and ground stations for seamless operations.

**Frustrations:**
- Delays due to unclear user interfaces or unresponsive systems.
- Inaccurate telemetry data leading to incorrect decision-making.
- Difficulty in segregating high-priority cargo due to complex data views.

## Persona 2: Samir Iqbal, Chief Software Architect

**Bio:**
Samir Iqbal is the chief software architect at UETF, responsible for overseeing the architectural design and implementation of the DEEP_SPACE platform. With a background in computer science and specialization in real-time systems, Samir thrives in creating scalable and secure environments. He values precision, efficiency, and innovation in software development and is enthusiastic about new technologies that can achieve these goals.

**Goals:**
- Build a scalable system that can handle real-time data processing and display with high fidelity.
- Ensure the application's architecture supports future enhancements and integrations.
- Maintain robust security protocols to protect classified data transmissions.

**Frustrations:**
- Performance bottlenecks that diminish the application's responsiveness.
- Compliance and security constraints that slow down development processes.
- Balancing innovative design with functional reliability and usability.

---

## Persona 3: Elise Tan, Security Operations Specialist

**Bio:**
Elise Tan is a dedicated security operations specialist with a focus on interstellar logistics. Her career in cybersecurity spans over 15 years, with experience in both governmental and private sectors. Elise is highly analytical, with a knack for spotting vulnerabilities in complex systems and devising strategic solutions to mitigate them.

**Goals:**
- Ensure that all data transmissions, particularly SOS alerts, are securely encrypted.
- Monitor the platform for potential security breaches and vulnerabilities.
- Develop protocols for secure cargo tracking and management, adhering to UETF's strict security standards.

**Frustrations:**
- Slow implementation of critical security updates due to bureaucratic delays.
- Difficulty collaborating with developers who prioritize features over security.
- Lack of transparency in data handling, leading to potential security oversights.

---

These personas provide a comprehensive view of the diverse users interacting with the DEEP_SPACE project, each with distinct goals, frustrations, and technical requirements crucial for the efficient design and development of the system.
